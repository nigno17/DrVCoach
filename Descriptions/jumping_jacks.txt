jumping_jacks description

1. Stand in front of the camera with your hands at your sides.

2. Jump up, simultaneously raising your straight arms from the
   sides and spreading your legs.

3. Land in a position with your legs spread apart and your arms
   pointing straight up.

4. Jump up again, bringing your arms back to your sides and
   closing your legs.

5. Land in the initial position: standing with your hands at
   your sides and legs closed.
